Poker Rules

Stream Together only (shared lobbies)

Poker mode is only available in multi-stream lobbies (codes starting with lobby-). Single-channel sessions stay on Blackjack.

Hand rankings (high to low)

  • Five of a kind (wildcards required)
  • Straight flush (royal flush is the top)
  • Four of a kind
  • Full house
  • Flush
  • Straight
  • Three of a kind
  • Two pair
  • One pair
  • High card

Betting basics

  • Ante: optional table-start contribution.
  • Actions: fold (drop), call, raise. If unchecked, you can check; sandbagging (check-raise) is allowed unless disabled.
  • Betting intervals: continue until bets are equalized; last interval ends with showdown.
  • Bluffing: if no one calls a bet/raise, bettor wins without showing.

Common limits

  • Fixed limit: bet/raise capped by street.
  • Pot limit: max bet is current pot size.
  • Table stakes: you can only wager whatâ€™s in front of you.
  • Raise cap: typically three raises per betting round.

Wild cards (if enabled)

House rules vary; common wilds include jokers/bugs, deuces, one-eyed jacks, or low hole card in stud. Five of a kind only exists when wilds are on.

Deal variants

  • Draw: all down cards; players may draw new cards (house rules).
  • Stud: some up, some down as betting progresses.

Etiquette

  • Follow written house rules; stick to posted time limits.
  • No private chip deals; banker handles chip buy-ins/outs.
  • Ties split pots; suits have no rank.

For deeper detail, see the in-app help or house rules posted by the host.
