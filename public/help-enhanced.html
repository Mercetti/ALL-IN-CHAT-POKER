Frequently Asked Questions

Is All-In Chat Poker free to use?

+

Yes! All-In Chat Poker is completely free for streamers. We offer optional cosmetic purchases in our store, but these don't affect gameplay and are purely for visual customization.

What are the system requirements?

+

Minimum requirements: Windows 10/macOS 10.14, 4GB RAM, modern browser (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge). For streaming: OBS 26+, stable internet connection, and a Twitch account.

How do viewers join the game?

+

Viewers join using chat commands like !join, !buyin, or !bet. They can also participate through the overlay interface if you enable it. No downloads required for viewers!

Can I customize the overlay appearance?

+

Yes! You can customize colors, positions, card styles, and more through the overlay editor. Premium cosmetics are also available in our store for additional customization options.

Is my data secure?

+

Absolutely. We use industry-standard encryption for all data transmission and never share your personal information. Only game-related data is stored, and you can delete it at any time.

Still need help?

Our support team is here to assist you with any questions or issues.
